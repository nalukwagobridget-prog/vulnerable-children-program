OUR STORY

The Vulnerable children program began in 2023 when our founder,NALUKWAGO BRIDGET, saw the urgent need for a safe haven and structured support for vulnerable children in kisenyi.

OUR MISSION

To protect, educate, and empower vulnerable children and youth in akawempe, providing the foundational support they need to overcome hardship and achieve self-sufficiency.

OUR MOTIVATION

We are motivated by the inherent potential and resilience of every child. We don't see victims; we see future leaders, artists, and innovators whose talent is simply waiting for a chance to emerge. Our work is a direct investment
